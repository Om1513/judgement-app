# Privacy Policy — Judgement (Kachuful)

**Effective date:** June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the **Judgement** mobile game
("the App," "we," "us") collects, how it is used, and your choices. We keep data
collection to the minimum needed to run online multiplayer games.

## Information we collect

The App does **not** require an account, email, or password. We collect only:

- **Display name** — the name you type to play. It is shown to other players in
  your lobby and game.
- **Device play ID** — a random identifier the App generates on your device
  (not your Apple ID, phone number, or advertising ID). It lets you rejoin your
  game after a disconnect or network change.
- **Gameplay data** — lobby codes, the players in a lobby, bids, cards played,
  turns, and scores, for the duration of a game.
- **Basic connection data** — a temporary connection (socket) identifier used
  while you are connected, so the right updates reach the right player.

We do **not** collect your real name, email, phone number, contacts, location,
photos, or precise device identifiers. The App contains **no advertising, no
analytics or tracking SDKs, and no third-party trackers.**

## How we use information

We use the information solely to:

- create and join game lobbies,
- run live multiplayer games and keep all players in sync,
- let you reconnect to a game in progress, and
- keep the service working and secure.

We do **not** sell or share your information for advertising or marketing, and
we do **not** use it to track you across other apps or websites.

## Where data is stored

- The display name and device play ID are stored **on your device** (so you
  don't have to retype them).
- Gameplay data is processed by our server and database, hosted by our
  infrastructure providers (Railway and Neon) on servers located in the United
  States. These providers process data on our behalf as service providers.

## Data retention

Gameplay data (lobbies, games, and associated player names) is transient and
kept only as long as needed to run and complete games; stale lobbies and games
are removed over time. You can clear locally stored data at any time by
deleting the App.

## Children's privacy

The App is a general-audience card game and is not directed to children under
13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you
believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will remove it.

## Your choices

- You can play using any display name you choose; it does not need to be your
  real name.
- Deleting the App removes the locally stored name and device play ID.
- To request deletion of any gameplay data associated with you, contact us.

## Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS) between the App and our server. No
method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable
measures to protect your information.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by updating the "Effective date" above.
